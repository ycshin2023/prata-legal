Privacy Policy

Prata · Last updated: 30 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains what information the Prata app ("Prata", "we", "us") collects when you learn Swedish with it, how we use it, and the choices you have. Prata is operated by Yeh Chul Shin. If you have any questions, contact us at ycshin2023@gmail.com.

Information we collect

  • Account — your email address, used to sign in with a one-time code (no password).
  • Learning progress — completed lessons, vocabulary you mark learned, spaced-repetition review schedules, quiz results, streaks, XP, levels and achievements.
  • Subscription status — whether you have an active Prata Pro subscription (managed by Apple and our subscription provider; we do not receive or store your payment-card details).
  • App preferences — interface language, theme, and your daily-reminder time. The daily reminder is a local notification scheduled on your device; we do not send push notifications from a server.
  • Voice conversations — when you use a live voice tutor or scenario, your microphone audio is streamed in real time to our speech provider to power the conversation. We do not record or store this audio.
  • Text conversations — messages you type to the AI coach are sent to our AI provider through our server to generate replies.

How we use it

  • To provide the lessons, vocabulary trainer, reviews, reading, and voice/text practice.
  • To save and sync your progress across sessions and devices.
  • To unlock paid content for active subscribers.
  • To send the optional daily practice reminder you choose to enable.

We do not show ads, and we do not sell your personal information.

Service providers

We share limited data with providers that operate parts of the app on our behalf:

  • Supabase — authentication, database and audio storage (your account and progress).
  • Apple & RevenueCat — processing and managing subscriptions.
  • ElevenLabs — text-to-speech and the real-time voice conversations.
  • Anthropic (Claude) — generating the AI coach's text replies.
  • Pexels — stock images shown with reading articles.
  • Expo — app delivery and on-device notifications.

Data retention & deletion

We keep your account and progress until you ask us to delete them. To delete your account and associated data, email ycshin2023@gmail.com from your registered address and we will remove it within 30 days.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU GDPR or California CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to certain processing. Contact us at the email above to exercise these rights.

Children

Prata is not directed to children under 13 (or under 16 where required), and we do not knowingly collect their data. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we will delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We will revise the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, provide notice in the app.
